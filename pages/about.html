Who We Are

RaZeX ESPORTS began as a grassroots BGMI team back in 2024. Over the years we’ve battled through countless top‑tier tournaments—securing multiple championship titles and clinching qualification spots for national events. Our players have proven their mettle on the biggest stages, from the Battlegrounds India Series (BGIS) to top tier tournaments.

Today, RaZeX ESPORTS evolves into a full-fledged organization—expanding our roster, infrastructure, and content creation. We’re dedicated to nurturing talent, pushing the BGMI competitive scene forward, and delivering high‑octane matches for our fans.
